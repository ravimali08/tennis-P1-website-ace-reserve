## 9. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
1. **What membership options are available?**
   * *Answer:* We offer Individual, Family, Junior, and Corporate memberships. Each includes court access, guest privileges, and discounted program rates. Contact us for a detailed comparison.
2. **Can non-members book courts or attend events?**
   * *Answer:* Many events and court bookings are open to non-members, often at a slightly higher rate. Members receive priority booking and discounted pricing.
3. **What should I bring to my first coaching session?**
   * *Answer:* Bring your racquet, appropriate court shoes, water, and comfortable athletic clothing. If you do not have a racquet, we have demo racquets available.
4. **How do tournament registrations work?**
   * *Answer:* Register online through our tournament pages. After payment, you will receive a branded tournament pass with a QR code and unique pass ID for check-in.
5. **Is there parking at the club?**
   * *Answer:* Yes, we offer complimentary parking for members and guests. Electric vehicle charging stations are also available.
6. **Do you offer private lessons?**
   * *Answer:* Absolutely. Private and semi-private lessons are available with all of our certified coaches. You can book through the contact page or member dashboard.
