## 3. Membership Pricing & Tiers
1. **Junior Membership** - *$99 / month*
   - Target: Players under 18.
   - Includes: Designated junior court slots, 10% program discounts, and junior club tournament access.
2. **Individual Membership** - *$189 / month*
   - Target: Core adult players.
   - Includes: 7-day court booking window, 2 complimentary guest passes/month, 15% lesson discounts, full fitness/locker access.
3. **Family Membership** - *$299 / month*
   - Target: Couples & families (up to 4 members).
   - Includes: 10-day booking window, 4 guest passes/month, 20% coaching/junior camp discounts, full fitness access.
4. **Elite Membership** - *$499 / month* (VIP Tier)
   - Target: High-performance competitors & VIPs.
   - Includes: 14-day priority bookings, 8 guest passes/month, 30% program discounts, 2 private coaching lesson credits/month, VIP private locker & lounge access.
